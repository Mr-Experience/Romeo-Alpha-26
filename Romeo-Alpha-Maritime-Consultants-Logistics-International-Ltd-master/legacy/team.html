Meet Our Leaders

The experienced professionals driving maritime excellence.

At Romeo Alpha, our success is built on the expertise, dedication, and leadership of our people. Our management team combines decades of international maritime experience with a forward-thinking approach to operations and logistics.

FOUNDER & VISIONARY

Chief Executive Officer

"Our journey is defined by the trust we build at sea and the excellence we deliver in every nautical mile."

With over three decades of visionary leadership in the global maritime industry, our founder established Romeo Alpha Maritime Consultancy with a singular goal: to redefine excellence in offshore support and logistics. Under his guidance, the company has grown from a specialized consultancy into a premier regional power, known for its uncompromising commitment to safety, integrity, and operational precision.

His deep understanding of international maritime law and coastal logistics has been instrumental in navigating the complex challenges of modern sea transportation and high-value asset protection.

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Capt. James Sterling

Capt. James Sterling brings an unparalleled wealth of experience to our daily operations. A master mariner with over 25 years of experience in deep-sea navigation and maritime operations management, he overseas the strategic direction and safety protocols of our entire fleet.

His leadership ensures that every mission—from complex anchor handling to high-stakes marine security—is executed with the highest degree of professionalism and technical accuracy.
